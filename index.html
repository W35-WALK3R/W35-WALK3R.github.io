About

I am a current junior Mechanical Engineering student with hands-on experience working across multiple industries, including the civil field, MEP systems, automotive maintenance, and the part manufacturing field. Throughout my internships and contract work, I have developed a strong ability to read, interpret, and produce technical drawings across fields — from structural drawings to MEP layouts to precision part schematics.

What sets me apart is my ground-up understanding: I have either shadowed or directly contributed to the design, diagnosis, and prototyping processes, giving me unique insight into how drawings and models translate into real-world results. Whether working with AutoCAD, Revit, SolidWorks, or physical tools, I always focus on design intent, practical feasibility, and real-world application. I am looking for opportunities to continue expanding my knowledge in any industry application of CAD.
